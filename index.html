📖 Details of the measures
  • 1. Deep sleep: Protocol so that all computers and smartboards are disconnected outside school hours to eliminate phantom consumption.
  • 2. Switch to LED: Progressive replacement of classic fluorescent tubes in classrooms with LED panels.
  • 3. Solar Panels: Utilization of self-consumption panels to cover the base demand of servers and fridges.
  • 4. Motion sensors: Installation of detectors in hallways and restrooms so the lights turn off automatically.
  • 5. Climate Adjustment: Set thermostats to 20°C in winter and 26°C in summer (if applicable).

📖 Details of the measures
  • 1. Digitization: Eliminating paper for circulars, using digital platforms (iEduca, Classroom).
  • 2. Copy restriction: Assignment of photocopy quotas per department and teachers using a PIN code.
  • 3. Refillable markers: Replacing single-use markers with ink refill systems.
  • 4. Paper reuse: Creation of "scrap paper" trays in all classrooms for drafts.
  • 5. Joint purchases: Centralizing purchases to order large formats and reduce unnecessary packaging.
